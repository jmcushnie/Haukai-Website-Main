Kia Ora

Welcome to Haukai Restaurant, we are located in the Bay of Islands where we offer a unique culinary experience that celebrates Māori culture and heritage. Our dishes are crafted with locally sourced, seasonal ingredients and infused with traditional Māori flavours and techniques. We invite you to explore our menu, learn about our culture, and make a reservation to join us for an unforgettable dining experience.

Our Menu

A range of traditional and contemporary dishes, crafted from the freshest local ingredients. Whether you're a local or a tourist, we invite you to join us for a dining experience that's both delicious and culturally enriching.
